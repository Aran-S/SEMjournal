About Us

Society for Nature and Applied sciences (SNAS) is a non-profit scientific and professional organization gathering experts interested in basic, natural, applied and other related sciences within the frame of research, education, and applications. Society for Nature and Applied sciences is a society fully devoted and dedicated to the advancement of nature and applied sciences. SNAS facilitate global co- operation in research, education and knowledge exchange. The motive of the society is to incorporate all national and cross-national academic institutions, research institutions, companies and individuals sharing interest in development of nature and applied sciences. We also support our members through a wide array of services, publications, organizing seminars/Conferences and other social events.

Science Engineering Medical (SEM) journals publishes original and review papers, surveys, experiments, case studies, technical reports, book reviews, notes, commentaries and news in various crop and applied sciences under the control of Society for Nature and Applied sciences (SNAS). Journal of Crop and Applied Sciences (JCAS) is a Double blind peer-reviewed scientific journal dedicated to publish high quality research work in the field of Crop and Applied Sciences agricultural sciences by SEM journals group. JCAS publishes article in Quarterly (March, June, September and December) basis without compromise the research quality and ethics. The Journal welcomes the submission of manuscripts that meets the general criteria of significance and scientific excellence.

Objectives/Activities
  • To develop a platform for integration of all research scholars, scientists, academicians, entrepreneurs, policy makers, and others interested in basic, nature and applied sciences for overall development of the nation.
  • To publish books, journals, proceedings and reports for advancement of research in basic, natural and applied sciences as approved by the executive committee
  • To conduct conferences, seminars, symposia and workshops for the basic, nature applied sciences community
