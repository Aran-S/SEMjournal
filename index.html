Welcome to SEM Journals

SEM journals are committed to maintain high standards for publications of any research findings through a rigorous peer-review together with strict ethical policies. Any infringements of professional ethical codes, such as plagiarism, fraudulent use of data, bogus claims of authorship, will be taken very seriously by the chief editor and editorial team members.

The journals accept manuscripts on the pretext that the submitted papers have not been published or under consideration for publication elsewhere. SEM journals are dedicated to provide rapid publication of original research, review articles, short communications on crop and applied sciences. Journal of Crop and Applied Sciences (JCAS) is an open access international peer-reviewed publication that gathers and disseminates fundamental and applied knowledge in almost all area of plant and animal bio-sciences.
